About Me

Experimental physicist specialising in surface dynamics, spectroscopy, and precision instrumentation. My research focuses on addressing fundamental questions in molecular-level interactions that are often inaccessible using existing scientific techniques. To overcome these limitations, my research group develops and applies advanced experimental methods, combining molecular beam scattering, laser spectroscopy, and high-precision diagnostic tools to explore reaction dynamics at surfaces.
